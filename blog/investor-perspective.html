Capital Allocation, ESG Tokens & Long-Term Value • June 2026

The 65/35 Hybrid Model

Grüner Mars enforces a strict allocation: 60–70% Earth regenerative biology (proven, lower risk) + 30–40% space-agriculture / advanced tech (higher upside, IP heavy).

DRI Slot 30 Benefits

  • Dedicated capital tranche within the DRI portfolio for biological infrastructure.
  • ESG-Spiral-Token backing directly linked to measurable biomarkers (soil health, biomass, O₂ output).
  • 20+ year investment horizon aligned with Mars mission timelines.
  • Knowledge IP from Mycotecture, PRB/MICP, and closed-loop systems has dual-use Earth value.

Risk / Return Profile

Lower technical risk than pure space-tech plays because core biology is Earth-derived and already scaling (permaculture, aquaponics). Upside comes from first-mover positioning in the emerging space-bio economy.
